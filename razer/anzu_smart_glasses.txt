# Replace Lenses

remove lens
 1. Turn to the back of the frame
 2. Apply firm pressure to the top of the lens
 3. push firmly until the lens pops out

add lens
 1. turn frame around and align new lens to the nose bridge corner
 2. press along the lens edge firmly to snap it into place

taps usually need to cover the whole front side of the frame. tapping your finger awkwardless side ways seems to work well
for all taps:
  front right side seems to work

# pause song
single tap on side of frame

# next song
double tap on side of frame

# prev song
triple tap on side of frame
